We fight together

We are humans

It hurts deeply to realize that despite the fact that this country is being known as ‘Agricultural nation’, thousands of our farmers are ending their lives. We have to accept that we failed short to predict the situation in advance and didn’t planned for any preventive action. Although now there can not be a sudden stop to all this, but we strongly feel that some small but immediate actions can definitely reduce the intensity of such incidents.

Instead of always pointing towards Government we believe that as a part of this society it is everyone's equal responsibility to help our farmers. There is so much to do and we cannot do all of that alone, but if every one of us do their own bit of responsibility then it will definitely lead to a big change.

The 'Mission Save Farmer' has to become people's self led movement. Let’s join hands to fight together, to save our farmers, the soul of our country.

We care for their future

We are humans

We strongly believe that if children are given good primary education and enough opportunities, they can figure out their own way out their own way to a brighter future.

It hurts deeply to realize that there are many school drop outs in rural area, especially in farmer families, in spite of the fact that seeking education is a fundamental right. While we cannot possibly solve all issues, we can at least help some of them with financing, resources and counseling.

There is so much to do in this space; but we currently focus on students from small villages in Maharashtra, doing as much as we can to enable these students to continue their schooling.

There are those that need help and there those who want to help, We act as a bridge connecting them.

Our Key Initiatives

MISSION SAVE FARMER

We support poor farmers, especially the families of deceased farmers, by furrowing their farms and providing quality seeds, fertilizer, pesticides, all at free of cost. This is to reduce the financial burden and prevent them from ending their own lives.
DONATE NOW

EDUCATIONAL AID

We support education of children of deceased farmers, orphan, physically challenged and poor family kids. We provide career counselling, and also motivate them towards competitive exams. It is one of our key initiative and being managed via Kartavya.
DONATE NOW

MEDICAL AID

We provide financial aid for farmers, old age people and other poor families, in case of any medical emergency. We also help physically challenged persons by providing them much needed supportive accessories to make their lives better.

MOTIVATION & GUIDANCE

We regularly organize guidance sessions of agricultural experts to motivate farmers to learn and adopt recommended techniques, strategies. Also the career guidance and motivational sessions are being conducted for their children.

About us

The Aadharwad Foundation is a registered society established to help poor and needy farmers, especially in the region of Marathwada, without restriction to any cast, community or religion.

It intends to help farmers morally, financially or in any other way, in order to prevent the farmers from committing suicide. It also provides financial assistance in case of medical or any other emergency in their families. Furthermore, it support the education of the children of needy farmers by providing them all required educational accessories, and financial aid for their fees.

Current Highlights

  • Assistance worth Rs. 1,25,000/- for 10 deceased (suicide committed) farmer's families for farming of this season.
  • Financial aid of worth Rs. 92,000/- for 8 needy families during their medical and other emergency situation.
  • Educational support of worth Rs. 4,50,000/- for more than 200 students for current academic year.
