STUDY CENTER

You can't change your past, but you can always change your future.

After independence as years passed the agriculture and villages have lost its importance for policy makers of India. We have almost lost the connect between policy makes and our villages - causing various issues that we have in rural these days. If we really want to see progress of our villages then this link has to be reestablished, and for that right people needs to enter in to decision making system of our government. Furthermore, we think that the people who have lived closely with these problems can suggest more effective solutions to these problems. However the required study material would not be affordable to everyone, especially children from farmer family. With this backdrop, On 16th July 2015 in presence of Hon. Shri. Rahul Maknikar (Additional Superintendent of Police, Jalna) we established a STUDY CENTER especially for the preparation for competitive exams. Today it is having enough study material for the preparation of MPSC and other government recruitment exams, all available at free of cost.

Partur, being a very small town, students studying there do not have any place dedicatedly available for study purpose. No need to tell the electricity problems in the region. In the form of Study Center somewhere we have also tried to remove that hurdle as well. Today large number of students are regularly utilizing it for their regular study purpose.

We regularly invite those who have been successful in these exams and proved themselves as role model for others, so that they can share their valuable experiences with these students and motivate them in order to achieve their own goals.
