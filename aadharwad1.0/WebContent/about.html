ABOUT US

Aadharwad Foundation - A Helping Hand.

Since 2007 we have been tirelessly working for education of children from rural area of Maharashtra, especially the kids of farmer families. In recent years there had been considerable increase in farmer suicides, and it deeply hurts when realized that the reason behind is not always uncontrollable. No doubt that State government is working towards a long term solution for this severe problem, however we strongly feel that some immediate actions can definitely reduce the intensity of such incidents. Instead of again pointing towards Government, we believe that as a part of this society it is everyone's equal responsibility to help those needy farmers. This small thought has ignited the establishment of Aadharwad Foundation, and we started our first 'Mission Save Farmer' initiative in December 2014.

Later in 2016, It has been legally registered as society (Reg. no. Maha-278/16) established to help poor and needy farmers, especially in the region of Marathwada, without restriction to any cast or religion. It intends to help farmers morally, financially or in any other way, in order to prevent them from ending their own lives. It will assist farmers to learn and adopt new farming strategy, techniques to improve their crops productivity, with reduced efforts. Additionally it will provides financial assistance in case of medical or any other emergency, with an intension to reduce the extra burden from their minds. While doing all this, the Kartavya (Reg. no. Maha-288/15) will continue to support education of their children by providing them all required educational accessories and supplementary financial aid.

There is so much to do in this space. We cannot do everything needed, but we are strongly determined to do everything that is possible for us to keep their hopes alive.

Aadharwad, A Helping Hand !
