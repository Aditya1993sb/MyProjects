MEDICAL ASSISTANCE

The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others.

In rural area the financial condition of few families is very weak, they can not even bear the expenses of their own health care. The financial crises force them to ignore their own health problems, and with passing time it becomes more serious - sometime it leads to death. It's very natural to have health issues in old age, but with regular medical treatments one can live a better life. Our farmers also understands this, but financial crises doesn't allow them to do so, and hence most of the old aged people in their family suffers hail lot of pain during their last days. A farmer seeing all this with his open eyes must be going through equal pain, right? If a farmer could not give better medical treatment for his children or parents, it makes him feel guilty and sometimes becomes the reason behind their suicide.

Having that in mind, we assist farmers, labors and other poor families caught in to any serious health issues, with whatever best possible financial help that we can do. In addition to that, We personally contact their doctor or hospital, to explain them the situation with a request to reduce the hospital's charges on humanity basis.

We also help physically challenged people by providing them much needed supportive accessories to make their lives better. In line with this, We have distributed special Guide Sticks for the Blind or Partially Sighted persons, special TriCycle for Handicapped persons. Yes, we do all that is possible and feasible for us.
